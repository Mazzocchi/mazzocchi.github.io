<h2>About</h2>


<p>


I am currently PhD student at <a href="http://www.ulb.ac.be/">ULB</a>


in the <a href="http://www.ulb.ac.be/di/verif/index-en.html">formal methods and verification</a> team


supervised by <a href="http://www.ulb.ac.be/di/ssd/filiot/">Emmanuel Filiot</a>


and <a href="http://www.ulb.ac.be/di/ssd/jfr">Jean-Fran&ccedil;ois Raskin</a>.




My work is funded by the competitive individual 4 year grant <a href="http://www.fnrs.be/en/">FNRS-FRIA</a>


and focuses on decidable and efficient formalisms for quantitative verification.


</p>




<p>


<u>Keywords</u>: counter machines, computational complexity, decidable logics, formal methods, game theory, model-checking, Presburger arithmetic, quantitative languages, system verification, transducers, weighted automata.


</p>


</section>
