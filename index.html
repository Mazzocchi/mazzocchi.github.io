About

I am a Post Doctoral researcher at the IMDEA Software Institute in Madrid under the direction of Pierre Ganty.

I did my PhD at ULB in the formal methods and verification team supervised by Emmanuel Filiot and Jean-François Raskin. My work is funded by the competitive individual 4 year grant FNRS-FRIA and focuses on decidable and efficient formalisms for quantitative verification.

Keywords: counter machines, computational complexity, decidable logics, formal methods, game theory, model-checking, Presburger arithmetic, quantitative languages, system verification, transducers, weighted automata.
